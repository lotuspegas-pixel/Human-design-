Arcana Profile

Discover your personal thinking style, feeling style and growth path.

Arcana Profile is a free, multilingual self-reflection tool. You answer 64 short statements and receive a warm, extensive personal reading based on eight archetypes — The Idea Finder, The Pattern Seer, The Action Maker, The Calm Keeper, The Plan Builder, The Clear Thinker, The People Helper and The Heart Listener — grouped into four colour clusters: Amber, Indigo, Karmijn and Jade.

Optionally, you can deepen your reading with a Human Design layer: from your date, time and place of birth we compute a real astronomical bodygraph (your type, authority, profile and definition) and merge it with your test results into one personal reading.

Available in English, Dutch, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Polish, Swedish and Turkish. Free to use, no account needed — everything stays on your own device. This is a self-insight instrument, not a diagnosis or selection tool.
